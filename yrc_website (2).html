About Us

Yorkshire Rose Care Ltd provides high-quality, person-centred supported living and domiciliary care for adults with mental health needs and learning disabilities. Our mission is to empower every individual to live independently, confidently and safely within their community.

Our Core Values

Proud

We take pride in the outcomes we help individuals achieve, ensuring dignity, respect and empowerment in all support provided.

Resilient

We remain strong, adaptable and solution-focused, supporting individuals through challenges with compassion.

Committed

We are dedicated to delivering safe, effective care that helps people thrive and live fulfilling lives.

Supported Living

We specialise in supporting adults with mental health needs and learning disabilities to live independently in safe, well-supported accommodation. Our support includes:

  • Daily living skills
  • Emotional and mental health support
  • Community access & confidence building
  • Managing appointments and medication prompts
  • Building independence and long-term outcomes

Domiciliary Care

Our domiciliary care service is designed to support people safely at home while promoting independence. We provide:

  • Personal care
  • Meal preparation
  • Medication prompts
  • Home tasks & routines
  • Community involvement
