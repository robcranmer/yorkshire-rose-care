About Yorkshire Rose Care

Yorkshire Rose Care Ltd is a locally-run care provider based in Bradford delivering Supported Living and Domiciliary Care across West Yorkshire. We specialise in supporting adults with mental health needs and learning disabilities. Our services focus on recovery, skill development, independence and keeping people connected to their communities.

We work closely with commissioners, social workers, families and health partners to deliver outcomes-led care that is safe, compassionate and effective. We are proud to be regulated by the CQC and maintain high standards of governance, training and safeguarding.

Supported Living Services

Our supported living programmes are personalised to help people live more independently in their own tenancies or shared homes. We support:

  • Tenancy sustainment and independent living skills
  • Shared living arrangements with tailored staffing
  • Assessment and goal-setting with regular reviews
  • Support for social inclusion, education and work

Domiciliary (Home) Care

Our domiciliary care services bring skilled carers into people’s homes to offer support with daily living and wellbeing. We provide:

  • Personal care, medication support and prompts
  • Support with appointments, shopping and community access
  • Flexible call times, waking nights and live-in options
  • Mental health informed approaches and positive behaviour support
