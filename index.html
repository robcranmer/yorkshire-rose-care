About Us

Yorkshire Rose Care Ltd was founded by two friends who shared one vision — to provide exceptional, person‑centred support to individuals with mental health needs and learning disabilities across West Yorkshire. With strong professional backgrounds and personal motivation, the service was built on compassion, quality, and a desire to make a meaningful difference.

We specialise in Supported Living and Domiciliary Care for adults who want to live independently while receiving the right level of support. Our approach is based on dignity, empowerment, and partnership. We work alongside individuals, families, and local authorities to ensure every person receives bespoke support designed around their unique strengths and needs.

We are proudly regulated by the Care Quality Commission (CQC) and committed to delivering safe, effective, and responsive care. Whether at home or in the community, our goal is simple: to help each person live the life they choose, with the support they deserve.

Our Values

  • Resilient – We stand strong through challenges and empower others to do the same.
  • Committed – We are dedicated to providing exceptional care, every day.

What We Offer

Supported Living

Flexible, person‑centred support helping individuals live independently within the community. We assist with daily living, wellbeing, mental health management, community engagement, developing skills and confidence, and maintaining a safe and stable home environment.

Domiciliary Care

Professional care delivered in people's own homes, including personal care, medication support, emotional support, household tasks, and daily living assistance — always delivered with dignity, respect, and compassion.
