About Us

Yorkshire Rose Care was founded to deliver meaningful, consistent and compassionate support to adults with mental health needs and learning disabilities across West Yorkshire.

We believe good care is built on trust, relationships and understanding. Our approach is flexible and person-centred, ensuring support fits the individual rather than the system.

We work alongside individuals, families and professionals to promote independence, emotional wellbeing and a sense of belonging within the community.

Our Values

Proud

We are proud of the people we support, their achievements, and the progress they make every day.

Pride for us means celebrating individuality, respecting identity, and recognising that every person has strengths, potential and value.

Resilient

We remain calm, consistent and supportive during times of challenge, change or uncertainty.

Resilience means standing alongside individuals through difficult moments and helping them develop coping strategies, confidence and emotional strength.

Committed

We are committed to delivering safe, high-quality support that consistently meets individual needs.

Our commitment shows in strong safeguarding, professional development, and a relentless focus on positive outcomes for those we support.

What We Offer

Domiciliary Care

Tailored home-based support promoting independence, safety and wellbeing.

Our care focuses on dignity, choice and consistent routines within familiar environments.

Supported Living

Support that enables people to live independently while achieving personal goals.

We help build life skills, confidence and community engagement.

Specialist Support

Trauma-informed support for individuals with complex needs.

Our approach promotes stability, emotional wellbeing and long-term outcomes.
