About Us

Yorkshire Rose Care was founded to bring genuine Yorkshire values into the care sector. Compassion, resilience, honesty and community spirit shape everything we do.

We support adults with mental health needs and learning disabilities across West Yorkshire. Our service focuses on helping people live independently, safely and confidently, with support tailored to their individual needs and goals.

We are proud of the lives we help transform and proud to serve the communities of Yorkshire.

What We Offer

Domiciliary Care

Our domiciliary care service provides personalised support to individuals who wish to remain in their own homes while receiving the help they need. We promote independence wherever possible, building routines that empower people to develop confidence and daily living skills.

Support may include personal care, medication prompts, meal preparation, wellbeing support, emotional reassurance and maintaining a healthy environment. Every care plan is designed collaboratively to prioritise dignity, respect and positive outcomes.

Supported Living

Our supported living provision enables individuals to live independently while receiving tailored, structured support. We focus on developing confidence, life skills and meaningful community connections, ensuring each person has control, stability and choice.

Support includes tenancy sustainment, budgeting, appointments, developing daily living skills and accessing meaningful activities. We aim to create safe and empowering environments where individuals can thrive with the right support in place.

Get in Touch

Specialist Support

Our specialist support is designed for people with mental health needs, learning disabilities and more complex personal challenges. We use compassionate and trauma informed approaches that promote emotional stability, coping skills and long term wellbeing.

Support includes recovery work, communication development, confidence building, community access, social inclusion and structured planning around personal goals. Every plan is strengths based and focused on individual aspirations.
