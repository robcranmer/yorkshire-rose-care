About Us

Yorkshire Rose Care Ltd was created with a passion for empowering individuals with mental health needs and learning disabilities. Our aim is to support people to live confidently, safely and independently within their own homes and communities.

We believe in person‑centred, flexible care that adapts to each individual. From emotional wellbeing to structured support, we focus on dignity, respect and meaningful outcomes.

Our service exists because everyone deserves opportunities, independence and compassionate support.

What We Offer

Domiciliary Care

Supported Living

Safe, structured support that encourages independence, builds confidence, and promotes community engagement.
