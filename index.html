Yorkshire Rose Care was created with a deep passion for supporting adults with mental health needs and learning disabilities. Our foundation is built on compassion, integrity and genuine community values that define the Yorkshire way.

We deliver personalised, empowering support that adapts to each person. Whether it's emotional wellbeing, independent living or community involvement, we focus on dignity and meaningful outcomes.

What We Offer

Domiciliary Care

We provide personalised home support that helps people remain independent and safe. Support includes daily living tasks, routines, emotional reassurance and tailored independence plans.

Everything we do is based on dignity, respect and meaningful engagement â€” empowering people at every step.

Supported Living

Through structured and flexible support, we help individuals develop skills, build confidence and maintain independence in a stable environment shaped around personal choice.

Our supported living ethos promotes community participation, growth and a strong sense of belonging.

Specialist Support

For individuals with complex needs, our trauma-informed, person-centred approach builds resilience, communication skills and emotional wellbeing.

Each support plan is personalised and strengths-focused, promoting recovery and meaningful development.
