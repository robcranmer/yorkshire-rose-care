About Us

Yorkshire Rose Care was founded to bring genuine Yorkshire values into the care sector. Compassion, resilience, honesty and community spirit shape everything we do. Our service is built on forming meaningful relationships and empowering people to take steps toward independence.

We support adults with mental health needs and learning disabilities across West Yorkshire, offering tailored care designed to promote wellbeing, confidence and stability. Everything we do is person centred, strengths based and focused on helping people live safely and independently.

We are proud of the lives we help transform and proud to serve the communities of Yorkshire.

What We Offer

Domiciliary Care

Our domiciliary care service provides personalised support to individuals who wish to remain in their own homes while receiving the help they need. We promote independence wherever possible, building routines that empower people to develop confidence and daily living skills.

Support may include assistance with personal care, medication prompts, meal preparation, wellbeing support, emotional reassurance and help with maintaining a healthy home environment. Every care plan is created collaboratively, focusing on dignity, respect and positive outcomes.

Supported Living

Our supported living service helps individuals live independently while receiving structured support tailored to their needs. We encourage choice, stability and personal growth, ensuring people feel safe, supported and in control of their lives.

This includes tenancy support, managing daily tasks, budgeting, attending appointments, developing life skills and taking an active role in the community. Our aim is to help people build meaningful connections and work towards long term independence.

Specialist Support

Our specialist support service is designed for individuals with mental health needs, learning disabilities and more complex challenges. We deliver compassionate, trauma informed care that promotes resilience, self awareness and wellbeing.

This support may include emotional regulation work, community engagement, confidence building, communication development, coping strategies and structured planning for personal goals. Every care plan focuses on strengths, empowerment and sustainable progress.
