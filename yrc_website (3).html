Our Story

Yorkshire Rose Care was founded by two lifelong friends with a shared passion for improving the lives of vulnerable individuals.

Robert Cranmer, a Mental Health Clinician and Care Manager for nearly 20 years, has dedicated his career to supporting adults with complex mental health needs.

Salman Khan, a dedicated parent, entered the care sector to make a meaningful impact and ensure high-quality support for those who rely on it.

Our Services

Domiciliary Care

Home-based support including daily living tasks, medication, companionship and independence-focused help.

Supported Living

Tailored support enabling adults to live independently while receiving structured, recovery-focused care.

Our Values

Compassion. Integrity. Dignity. Empowerment. Every interaction reflects our commitment to meaningful care.
